⚙ settings & powerups
📜 how to play
The round. Drop bombs (your bomb key) to break walls and damage opponents. Each player has ❤️ 3 HP by default — a blast costs one heart, with a brief invulnerability window after. Survive when others run out of hearts. Bombs chain-detonate if caught in another's blast.
The draft. Between every round, all three players see three cards each. Use your left/right keys to highlight a card, your bomb key to confirm. Once everyone has confirmed, the next round starts. Card effects persist for the entire match.
Powerups (dropped from soft walls):
  • B more bombs — you can have +1 bomb on the board at once.
  • F longer blast — your blast radius grows by one tile.
  • S faster speed — +1 tile/sec movement.
  • K kick bombs — walk into a stationary bomb to push it. The bomb slides in your direction until it hits a wall, another bomb, or a player. (No separate "throw" button.)
  • D detonator — press your detonate key (P1=E, P2=O, P3=.) to manually blow up your oldest bomb instead of waiting for its fuse.
  • P flame pass — immune to your own blasts (still vulnerable to other players' bombs).
  • I brief invuln — a few seconds of full immunity on pickup.
Rune bombs (from the card draft):
  • ✦ Cluster rune — every bomb you drop spawns 4 short-fuse child bombs at its cardinal neighbors after detonating. Massive coverage; easy to chain into yourself.
  • → Pierce rune — your blasts pass through soft walls, destroying multiple in a line. Range is what matters, not what's between you and them.
  • Your active rune is shown on each bomb (✦ or →) and as an indicator next to your hearts in the HUD.
