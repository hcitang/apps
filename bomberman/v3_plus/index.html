⚙ settings & powerups
📜 how to play — champion abilities
Hearts & bombs. 3 hearts per life. Standard bombs deal 1 heart. Cluster runes (from soft-wall powerups) drop a main bomb plus 4 minis — but each cluster bomb only deals ½ heart. Pierce blasts go through soft walls but are capped at 3 tiles per direction.
Champions (pick once per game):
  • 💣 SuperBomber — double-tap your detonate key to drop a SUPER-BOMB that fires immediately. Cross-blasts the entire row + column, obliterating every soft and inner hard wall (boundary is safe). Deals 2 hearts. Bombbomb himself is immune to it. 60 s cooldown.
  • 🌀 Teleporter — detonate to teleport to a random safe corner. 60 s cooldown.
  • ✂️ DefuseMan — detonate to defuse every bomb on your row and column instantly. 60 s cooldown.
  • 💪 StrongMan — walk into any wall (hard or soft) to shove it one tile in your direction, as long as the tile behind is empty. Max 3 pushes per rolling window (window length adjustable; default 10 s).
  • 🧛 Vampire — detonate to enter an aura state for 5 s; any other player you touch loses all their powerups. Super-fast while active. 60 s cooldown.
  • 😱 LavaMan — detonate to sprint mode for 2 s, leaving a lava trail behind you. He's immune to it while laying plus a 1 s grace window after, so he can step off the last tile safely. Lava persists 5 s. 60 s cooldown.
  • ❄ FreezeMan — detonate to fire a cross-shaped freeze ray through hard walls to the edges of the map. Bright icy flash visible for ~1 s. Anyone in the row/column is frozen 5 s. 60 s cooldown.
  • 😐 Loser — no ability. Just vibes.
  • 🎲 Random — pick this and the game rolls a champion for you.
Cooldown ring. Each champion's cooldown is shown as a ring above their head and as a fill bar on the side panel — full when ready. Every round starts with a 15 s lockout for all abilities. Active windows (Vampire aura, LavaMan trail) show a colored glow.
