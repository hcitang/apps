😀 Emojiman

faithful classic, party-game feel

Pure emoji on a pastel grid. Drop bombs, dodge blasts, pick up powerups, last one standing wins the round.

Twist: none — the control. Validates the engine.
v1.5 · playable

🎯 TileTap

deliberate, chess-with-bombs cadence

Discrete tile-by-tile movement. You can't hold a direction — release and tap again to keep moving. Every step is a commitment.

Twist: edge-detected input. No analog walking.
v3 · playable

🕯️ CRYPT

dungeon roguelite

Torchlit halls. 3 hearts per life instead of one-shot kills. Rune bombs (standard / cluster / pierce) carried per player.

Twist: between every round, all 3 players draft a card simultaneously — cards persist for the match.
v3+ · playable

🕯️ CRYPT+

champion select brawler

Crypt with 8 champions — SuperBomber's full-map super bomb, Teleporter, DefuseMan, StrongMan who shoves walls, Vampire who drains powerups on contact, LavaMan's trail, FreezeMan's cross-ray, plus Loser & Random.

Twist: pick once at game start. Half-heart cluster damage. Pierce capped at 3 tiles.

Retired

v2 · retired

BOMBWAVE

neon synthwave arcade

Vector glow, scanlines, perspective grid. Hold a direction while dropping a bomb to throw it — bombs slide until they hit something.

Twist: throwable bombs + lava trickles in from the edges, tile-by-tile.
v2.5 · retired

BOMBWAVE · TAP

discrete-move synthwave

Same neon arena, same throwable bombs, same creeping lava — but movement is one-tile-per-tap. Bombwave meets TileTap.

Twist: tap-to-move + throw-on-hold + lava creep, all combined.
