What is this

A public viewer for the CHI 2026 keyword-system redesign. Explore how authors actually used keywords across 6,730 submissions, versus how the existing controlled vocabulary captured them, and the proposed pile/sub-pile reorganization.

Author keywords are normalized (lowercase / trim), synonym-mapped (llm→large language models, etc.), then embedding-clustered with all-MiniLM-L6-v2 at cosine threshold 0.18, hand-audited, and arranged into a 2-level taxonomy of piles and sub-piles.

Read-only viewer. The working curation board lives elsewhere.
