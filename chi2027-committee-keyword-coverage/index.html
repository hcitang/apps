CHI 2027 — Keyword Coverage

Balance-first SC-recruiting tool. Four visual variations sharing one loaded committee. Data stays in your browser (sessionStorage) — nothing is uploaded.

What the numbers mean

supply — how many of your confirmed SCs cover this sub-pile. With K=R it's just the headcount; with R>K each Knowledgeable counts ½ of a Recently-published. Shown as supply (R·K) so you see the split.

demand — 2026 keyword volume: how many times terms in this sub-pile were tagged across last year's submissions. A proxy for how much work lands here. (d in the tiles.)

target / balance (default rule) — your people, spread in proportion to demand. target = (this sub-pile's share of total demand) × (total people in the facet). ratio = supply ÷ target: 1× = right share, <1× = thinner than its demand deserves, >1× = piled up. This is relative — it asks "are we balanced", and re-balances as you add SCs.

slots (other rule) — a fixed target of 1 / 2 / 3 covering SCs by demand tier (low / med / high). Simpler, absolute, fillable; the ?-heads show the shortfall. Saturates once you have many broad SCs — useful at small sets and later for AC/reviewer depth.

Colors (thin → piled) are bands of the ratio under whichever rule is active.
